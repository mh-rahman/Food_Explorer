What we do

It is in human nature to explore the world in search of food to satisfy their hunger. Whereas the foodies are in constant search for their next destination to satisfy their insatiable desire for food. We at Food Explorer intend to help these foodies find their next stop.


Why we do

Average Rating of a restaurant has been a conventional and principal measure based on which a decision is made. Ratings provide an overview picture of the restaurant and hence doesn't really serve the purpose of a foodie, as they are really interested about the food, plus a foodie traveling to a different state is unaware of the food trend and famous local food items.


How we do

Using techniques like Named Entity Recognition and Sentiment Analysis, we at Food Explorer, solve this concern by grasping the sentiment of the user's review and incorporating it in context of mentioned food. We suggest various restaurants based on on food trends in a given particular state, thereby providing a best food experience
